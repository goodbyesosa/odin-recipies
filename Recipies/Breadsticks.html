Breadsticks

This recipie will show you how to make breadsticks just like Olive Garden's.

These are the perfect side dish to all your favorite soups. And you just can’t go wrong with that classic soup, salad and breadsticks for dinner combo.

Ingredients

  • ALl Purpose Flour
  • Active dry yeast
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Salt
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Butter
  • Garlic Powder

Steps

  1. Proof yeast: To start dissolve yeast in warm water with 1/2 tsp sugar. Let rest and activate 10 minutes
  2. Mix in remaining sugar, a portion of flour, salt and oil: Add in granulated sugar, 1 1/2 cups flour, 1 3/4 tsp salt and vegetable oil, then blend with an electric stand mixer fitted with the paddle attachment until combined.
  3. Switch to hook attachment, add remaining flour and knead: Add remaining 1 1/2 cups flour and knead mixture on low speed, adding up to 1/4 cup additional flour as needed, and knead until dough is smooth and elastic.
  4. Transfer and let dough rise: Transfer dough to greased bowl, cover and rest 1 1/2 hours.
  5. Divide into 12 pieces, shape into a rope: Punch risen dough down, divide into 12 equal portions. Roll each piece into a 9 inch rope on a lightly floured surface, then transfer to lined baking sheets.
  6. Let rise a second time: Cover and let rise 1 hour. Preheat oven to 425 degrees near the end of rising.
  7. Bake: Bake 11 – 13 minutes until golden then brush with butter, sprinkle with salt and garlic powder.
